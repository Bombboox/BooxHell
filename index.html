Controls

Everything you need to survive for at least a few extra seconds.

Mouse
Move your character with the mouse.
Left Click
Hold to fire while the fight is active.
Space
Skip the boss countdown and spawn them immediately.
Escape
Return to the main menu from a fight or any menu screen.
R
Restart after a game over without returning to the menu first.
